Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1. Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

    Yes, we highly recommend booking your appointment in advance to ensure availability. Walk-ins are welcome but not guaranteed.

  • 2. What types of services do you offer?

    We specialize in customized facial treatments and professional waxing services for all skin types. Whether you’re looking to treat acne, aging, hyperpigmentation, or simply want to maintain healthy skin, we have options for you.

  • 3. How should I prepare for my facial or waxing appointment?

    For facials, come with a clean face if possible. Avoid exfoliating or using retinoids 24 hours before your appointment. For waxing, let hair grow to at least 1/4 inch and avoid sun exposure or tanning the area beforehand.

  • 4. What skincare products do you use?

    We use only top-rated, professional skincare brands chosen for their safety, quality, and effectiveness. Our estheticians can also recommend products best suited for your skin after your treatment.

  • 5. What if I have sensitive skin or allergies?

    Please let us know during booking or before your service. Our treatments are always customized, and we take great care to use products that are safe and gentle for sensitive skin.

  • 6. What is your cancellation policy?

    We ask that you provide at least 24 hours’ notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. No-shows or late cancellations may be charged a cancellation fee.

  • 7. Are your estheticians licensed and certified?

    Yes! All of our estheticians are fully licensed, trained, and experienced in professional skincare and waxing services.

  • 8. Can I buy gift cards or book services for someone else?

    Absolutely. Gift cards are available for any service or custom amount and make a great gift. You can also book appointments on behalf of a friend or loved one.

  • 9. Do you offer memberships or packages?

    We occasionally offer treatment packages, specials, or seasonal promotions. Ask during your visit or follow us on social media to stay updated.
