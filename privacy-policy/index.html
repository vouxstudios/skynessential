Privacy policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2014

 

At Skyn Essential, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly and securely. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you visit our website, book an appointment, or interact with our services in any way.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, date of birth, billing address, and other details you provide when booking services or completing forms.
  • Appointment Details: Service history, skincare preferences, allergies, or health information relevant to your treatment.
  • Payment Information: Securely collected through third-party payment processors.
  • Website Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited—collected via cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect may be used to:

  • Schedule and manage appointments
  • Personalize treatments and skincare plans
  • Process payments and send confirmations or invoices
  • Communicate promotions, updates, and special offers (only with your consent)
  • Improve our website, services, and client experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. Access to your information is restricted to authorized Skyn Essential team members and secure third-party providers as needed to deliver services.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted third-party vendors (such as payment processors or booking platforms) solely for the purpose of operating our business and serving you effectively.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and review the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections or updates to your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal and operational requirements)

To make a request, please contact us directly using the contact information below.

6. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and gather anonymous traffic data. You can adjust your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website or communication materials may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites and recommend reviewing their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
