Facials

Signature Facial

A customized, all-in-one treatment designed to cleanse, exfoliate, nourish, and rejuvenate your skin. Perfect for first-time clients or those seeking regular maintenance.
Suitable for all skin types.

Deep Cleansing Facial

Targets congested pores and breakouts with a deep cleanse, exfoliation, extractions, and purifying mask. Ideal for oily, acne-prone, or problematic skin.

Hydration Boost Facial

A replenishing facial designed to restore moisture balance, soothe dryness, and leave your skin feeling soft, plump, and radiant.
Perfect for dry, sensitive, or dehydrated skin.

Anti-Aging Facial

Focuses on fine lines, loss of elasticity, and dullness. Includes exfoliation, firming massage, collagen-boosting products, and a lifting mask to leave skin looking youthful and refreshed.

Brightening Facial

Designed to even skin tone, reduce dark spots, and boost your natural glow using vitamin C and brightening ingredients.
Great for hyperpigmentation and dull skin.

Acne Control Facial

A targeted treatment to calm inflammation, extract impurities, and prevent future breakouts. Includes antibacterial ingredients and soothing masks.
Best for acne-prone or congested skin.

Gentlemen’s Facial

Tailored specifically for men’s skin, this facial addresses razor irritation, ingrown hairs, and oil control while deeply cleansing and hydrating the skin.
